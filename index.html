How to read this desk: the engine scores fourteen technical signals (trend, momentum, mean-reversion, breakout, divergence, volume) into one long/short score per timeframe, then builds an ATR-based trade plan — entry, stop, target, hold window and position size from your capital and risk budget. It measures probabilities, not certainties: no model predicts markets. Never risk money you can't afford to lose.

⚙ Engine settings — every knob is yours

These settings drive everything — verdict labels, alerts, trade tickets AND the backtester. Tune them, then hit "Run backtest" to see how your configuration would have performed before you trust it. Strong signals automatically get +1.0 ×ATR extra target.

🤖 Auto-Trader AI — scans every chart, calls the shots

An expert system managing its own account: $100 starting capital, compounding. Every refresh it sweeps all 4 markets × 5 timeframes at real-time prices, reads the regime (aggressive in strong trends, defensive half-size and STRONG-only in chop), and sizes by conviction — 1% of equity risked at 50% conviction up to 2% at 90%+, never more, always with a stop and a minimum 1:2 reward:risk. It learns: every closed trade is reviewed into the journal below, wins and losses update its per-setup memory, and that record feeds back into the rankings — setups that keep losing get down-ranked, earners get up-ranked. Deterministic and auditable, paper-only by design: no web page should hold real exchange keys.

Backtest — proving the engine on history

Replays the exact same signal engine bar-by-bar over the fetched history with zero lookahead: entries fill on the next bar's open, and when a bar touches both stop and target the stop counts first (the conservative assumption). Excludes fees, funding and slippage — live results will be somewhat worse.
